KJ Systems India Private Limited is a healthcare product-based software company primarily focused on the US healthcare industry.

Company Overview

Founded in 2008 and incorporated as a Private Limited company in March 2015, we have grown into a global tech provider. With headquarters in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, we specialize in medical software development, data processing, and consultancy.

Our work culture is built on inclusivity, providing significant opportunities to freshers and offering permanent work-from-home options for many roles to ensure a healthy work-life balance.

Our flagship product, EHR Your Way, is a specialized Electronic Health Record software designed for medical practitioners and hospitals. It features a Dynamic Report Builder that allows healthcare providers to create custom diagnostic reports by pulling data from multiple modules effortlessly.
